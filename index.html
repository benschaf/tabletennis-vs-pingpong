A couple of differences

Ping pong and table tennis are two different names for the same sport. However, there are some differences between the two.

Ping Pong

Ping pong is a casual game that is often played in homes and recreational centers. It is played on a smaller table, and the paddles are usually made of wood. There is a sport called Clickball, but it is not very popular yet.

Table Tennis

Table tennis, on the other hand, is a professional sport that is played on a larger table. The paddles used in table tennis are made of rubber, and the game is played at a much faster pace than ping pong.

Timeline

this is how it happened

Victorian England

The sport originated in Victorian England

The sport was played among the upper-class as an after-dinner parlour game.

1860s

British military officers develop makeshift versions of the game in India

The officers brought the game back with them. A row of books stood up along the center of the table as a net, two more books served as rackets and were used to continuously hit a golf-ball.

1901

James W. Gibb discovers novelty celluloid balls on a trip to the US

Gibb found them ideal for the game. This was followed by E.C. Goode who, in 1901, invented the modern version of the racket by fixing a sheet of pimpled, or stippled, rubber to the wooden blade.

1902

An unofficial world championship was held

Table tennis was growing in popularity by 1901 to the extent that tournaments were being organized, books were being written on the subject, and an unofficial world championship was held in 1902. In those early days, the scoring system was the same as in lawn tennis.

1921

A new Table Tennis Association was founded

Although both a "Table Tennis Association" and a "Ping Pong Association" existed by 1910, a new Table Tennis Association was founded in 1921, and renamed the English Table Tennis Association in 1926.

1926

The International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF) was founded

The ITTF followed in 1926. London hosted the first official World Championships in 1926.

1988

Table tennis was introduced as an Olympic sport

Table tennis was introduced as an Olympic sport at the Olympics in 1988.
